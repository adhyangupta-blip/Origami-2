About us

In 2030, Artificial Intelligence and Robotics will touch 800 Million jobs. The world will split into creators and consumers. WhiteHat Jr makes kids creators in the new world.

Kids learn logic, structure, sequence, and algorithmic thinking to generate creative outcomes like animations and apps. All classes are taught via a Live Online platform connecting top certified teachers to children.

Launched in 2018, WhiteHat Jr is one of the fastest growing EdTech businesses in India, now expanding fast into global markets, including the US, UK, LatAm, Australia, and Indonesia.
